About Me

My full name is Benjamin Lewis Miller. I was born on September 12, 1996 in St. Louis, Missouri. I lived there for a few years before moving to Alpharetta, Georgia, where I was raised. I have one younger brother named Max,two cats, and one dog. My father's name is Ty and my mother's name is Bonnie.I attended Creekview Elementary School. I grew up playing sports so I have been a life long athlete. I played baseball, football, and basketball starting at a young age. After a few years, I found that my favorite of the three was basketball.
